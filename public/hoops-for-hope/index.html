Our Mission

A fundraiser built around perseverance.

The 18 Hours of Hope Free Throw Challenge is the inaugural signature fundraiser for Hopefull Collective — a powerful visual and emotional challenge symbolizing perseverance, resilience, and the power of community support.

Throughout the day, the gym becomes a community gathering focused on mental wellness, endurance, and collective support. Supporters, sponsors, and community members participate through pledges, sponsorships, competitions, and interactive experiences.

Every basket made represents hope, encouragement, and impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hopefull Collective?

Hopefull Collective is a nonprofit focused on mental wellness, resilience, and community support. The 18 Hours of Hope Free Throw Challenge is our inaugural signature fundraiser.

Who is Drew?

Drew is the founder and executive director of Hopefull Collective, a pastor, and a deeply rooted member of the Leesburg and Loudoun community. He understands the weight of mental health through his own personal journey — and he's passionate about helping others find hope. The 18 Hours of Hope challenge is his way of using perseverance, endurance, and the simple act of stepping to the free throw line again and again to raise funds and awareness for the cause he's built his life around.

Do I have to be there in person to participate?

Not at all. You can donate, pledge per shot, or sponsor an hour from anywhere. The full event will be livestreamed on YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook Live. In-person experiences (rebounder hours, breakfast, dinner) require attendance.

How do "Give Drew a Break" purchases work?

When you purchase a 5- or 15-minute break, that time is added to Drew's break bank for the day. Breaks are limited so the integrity of the challenge stays intact, but he'll absolutely thank you on the livestream.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes — Hopefull Collective is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You'll receive a receipt for tax purposes via email immediately after donating.

Can my company sponsor an hour?

Absolutely. Hour sponsorships are $1,000 each and include logo placement, livestream recognition, and a social media thank-you reel. Pick the hour that aligns with your audience above, or email us about custom packages.

How do I sign up to be Drew's rebounder?

Click "Pick Your Hour" in the Rebounder card or the Schedule section, choose an open slot, and submit your info. We'll confirm via email within 24 hours and send the payment link.

What if I want to volunteer instead of donate?

We need volunteers for scorekeeping, donation tracking, hospitality, and the livestream team. Email us at hello@hopefullcollective.org with "Volunteer" in the subject.
